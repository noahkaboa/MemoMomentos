Six Flags

May 19, 2024 - Cream Ridge, NJ

We went to Six Flags and had so msuch fun. I enjoyed dining at the world-renowned Fatburger for the low low price of $21. We went on a variety of rollercoasters, many of which involved being turned upside down. I particularly enjoyed consuming a Sprite-infused cup of Dippin' Dots. Although I cannot confidently say I enjoyed my company (namely Jeffer), I did have a very thrilling experience.
